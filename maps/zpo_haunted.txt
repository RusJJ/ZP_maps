                            Storyline
----------
You and your fellow survivors have found a scientist who claims to be able to make an antivirus, protect him while he works on it.

Objectives
----------

1. Defend the Scientist
2. Grab the antivirus
3. Escape

Hints
-----
- Keep the generator fueld if you want the traps to stay on
- If you need more guns or health kits fuel the generator on the second floor then use the computer to call for a supply drop
- Zombies can enter through a lot of different entrances keep your ears open.
- Be sure to have someone always defending the scientist
