Description
-----------
Your group of survivors has stumbled across a small farm after fleeing the initial outbreak of the infection in a nearby town.  Scavenge the buildings in the area for weapons and supplies and try to meet up with the other group of survivors across the farm.  Cooperate to find a defendable area and make your stand against the zombie horde!

Map Info
-----------

Original map Reanimated 

Max players: 24
Recommended: 8 - 24
