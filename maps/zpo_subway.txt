Description
-----------
You and a group of survivors decend into the city's subway system. Days pass by and the station is no longer safe. The group decides to gather supplies and escape by train.

Objective
-----------
Escape the subway system by train

1. Find the generator key
2. Activate the generator (2 min)
3. Break into maintainance room 
4. Activate secondary generator (1 min), 
5. Unlock platform 1 track door (at security room)
6. Activate train at generator room
7. Escape!
