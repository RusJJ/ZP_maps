Your boat has finally given up, but you have not.  The situation in this town looks grim.  Luckily there are clues that a safehouse is nearby.  Let's find it!

SURVIVORS:
----------

1) Break your way into the shipping office.
2) Open the large warehouse.
3) Destroy the fusebox to make the cargo container fall
4) Cross over the fallen cargo container into the upper floor
5) Get to the street
6) Push the generator to the safehouse
7) Seal the doors to the safehouse!


ZOMBIES:
--------

Your goal as a zombie is to stop the survivors from doing any of these things, including breathing.
